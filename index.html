FLOODIE

INTRODUCTION

Floods are the most common natural disaster in the world. The main reason for flooding is the heavy rain. Sri Lanka also faces floods almost in every year.

Floods cause great damage not only to human lives but also properties. Due to this, Sri Lankan government spends a lot of money to provide relief to those affected by the floods. The main reason for this situation is, the lack of a proper system for predicting rainfall and floods in Sri Lanka and informing the public about it.

To this issue, floody proposes to create a proper weather forecasting and water level forecasting system and visualize the flooding areas in a google map. And it propose a chatbot to give significant weather data to the public.

The advancement of technology and resources in today's world is expanding by the day. Science and technology can be used to forecast the weather in a particular location. Because of the complexity of the physical processes involved and the variability of rainfall in place and time, weather forecasting has been a tough issue in hydrology. Flooding can result in a variety of losses, including loss of life or injury, property loss, settlement damage, irrigation system damage, and road damage. Machine Learning Algorithms have been frequent and successful in water-related studies in recent years.
